Our Company

Indie gaming refers to video games developed and published by independent developers or small teams without the backing of major publishers or studios. Indie games are typically created with a limited budget and resources, but they often offer unique and innovative gameplay experiences that can't be found in big-budget titles. Indie games can be found on a variety of platforms, including PC, consoles, and mobile devices. They cover a wide range of genres, from puzzle games to RPGs, platformers, and more. Many indie games are known for their artistic and experimental qualities, featuring distinct art styles, innovative game mechanics, and unconventional narratives.

Our Gaming Scene

The indie gaming scene has grown rapidly in recent years, thanks in part to the rise of digital distribution platforms like Steam, Itch.io, and the App Store. These platforms have made it easier for independent developers to reach a wider audience and compete with larger studios. Overall, indie gaming offers a rich and diverse landscape of games that are often overlooked by mainstream media. It's a great way to discover new and exciting gaming experiences while supporting independent developers who are pushing the boundaries of what's possible in video games.
